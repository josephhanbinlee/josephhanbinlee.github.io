Survey Results Analysis

  • Apple Music customers tend to prioritize how quickly music is released more than Spotify customers.
  • Spotify customers are generally more satisfied with their curated playlists than those of Apple Music customers.
    • Along with that, Apple Music customers are less likely to use their streaming service to discover acts than Spotify customers.
    • This makes sense because Apple Music customers tend to be more confident about what kind of music they would want to download.
  • Customers of both platforms tend to have generally neutral opinions about listening to "lesser-known Korean artists."
  • On the other hand, customers of both platforms also consider how extensive the song catalog is when choosing their platform.
    • In fact, customers of both platforms both shared their dissatisfaction in how their streaming services tend to fall short of offering a greater catalog.

T E S T I M O N I A L S

Shared Issues

Difficulty finding songs

  • "The Korean songs are never listed with their original Korean titles, and are thus hard to recognize."
  • "All the songs are in English so sometimes I can’t find certain songs."
  • "...just sometimes harder to find artists or songs that aren't very well-known."
  • "Having a better search engine. It’s almost impossible to find [K-pop] songs on Apple Music."
  • "All the songs are in English so sometimes I can’t find certain songs."

Lacking discography

  • "More lesser-known artists' songs available, more of extensive (complete) availability of artists' discography."
  • "More older content (Ex: SNSD's older stuff is not on Spotify)."
  • "...the [K-pop] playlists on Spotify are still pretty mainstream in my opinion. I rarely see for example Kpop indie or even hip-hop on there!"
  • "[M]ore underrated artists would be nice! [Spotify] lacks variety when it comes to some artists!!!"
  • "More variety in older Korean music, classic songs from the 90s."
  • "Apple Music did not have lesser known music that I enjoy."
  • "I also find some artists [on Spotify] I couldn't find on Apple Music."

Advantages

Spotify

  • Better connectivity with other social media platforms
    • “I like to share my music playlists with my friends and more of them have Spotify”
    • "...[There are] also more options to connect to other platforms/sites (like [Instagram])"

Apple Music

  • Faster release time
    • "Some artists/albums are not immediately available on Spotify."
    • "...[Apple Music] is faster unless the artist specifically uses [Spotify] as the primary output."

Disadvantages

Spotify

  • Slower release time
    • “...and songs don't usually come out timely.”
    • "[New] songs need to be uploaded more quickly."
    • "The time it takes for a new Kpop release to become available needs to be shorter."

Apple Music

  • Interface / playlist curation issues
    • "Better curated K-pop playlists, better interface"
    • "Easier to download and curate playlists"
    • "...[Apple Music] seems overwhelming that it is connected to iTunes and that there's an action of syncing and buying."

Campaign Ideas

  • Spotify Sessions/Singles for Korean Artists
    • K-pop artists most usually come to the States, especially during events such as large-scale events such as KCON, making them available for creating such content.
    • Many artists already upload low-budget videos of themselves covering songs on their official social media outlets such as Twitter and Instagram. Giving them this platform would enable them to create covers that are of higher production quality.
    • Better promotion of these songs themselves would enable users to be notified of this sort of exclusive content.

  • More Social Media Connectivity Options
    • Currently, Spotify is more compatible with other social media platforms, particularly Instagram and Facebook Messenger.
    • Making these compatibilities available with other social media platforms would probably give it a slight upper hand compared to Apple Music, especially since there seems to be a rising trend in users sharing playlists.

  • Considering User Opinions
    • Taking the suggestions from users (i.e. utilizing Korean titles for songs, better search engine, etc) would also resolve the factors that make Spotify less appealing to them.
    • Furthermore, making more users aware of these changes would be important in convincing them to start using/switching to Spotify
