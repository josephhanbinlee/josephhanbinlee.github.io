ABOUT ME

Greetings from afar! My name is Joseph Lee, a sophomore studying Integrated Digital Media at NYU.

Though I was born in Texas, growing up in the metropolitan areas of Seoul, South Korea, has opened me to the wonders of Korean culture that are often so unknown in the States, compelling me to pursue a career in filmmaking and digital marketing in order to further promote the wave of East Asia's cultural products, particularly in the realms of entertainment and the arts, to the West.

From music videos to mockumentaries, arthouse films to YouTube comedies, if it's a story I want to tell, then I push through to make my artistic visions a reality.
