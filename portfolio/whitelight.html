white light

This was a piece I created in response to an artwork I saw and analyzed at the MoMA titled "White Light" by Jackson Pollock. I wanted to recreate the onslaught of visuals and textures that the original painting itself had as a metaphor for how the different visuals I've seen in my life inspired me as an artist.
