About Rainbow Christian Learning Center

Founded in 2011, Rainbow Christian Learning Center is a pre-school for children ages 2-4. Named after the rainbow, which symbolizes God's love and covenant of salvation meant for all people, Rainbow Christian Learning Center is an institution that holds onto that promise and assisting its students realize their given talents and God’s plan for them by helping them experience God’s love and become God’s promised children through faith in salvation by Jesus Christ.
