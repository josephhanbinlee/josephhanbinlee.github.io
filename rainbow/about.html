About the Director

Dr. Hyeyoung Lee, D. Min

Dr. Hyeyoung Lee, D. Min has worked in the Christian education field for over a decade. Along with her divination, she currently has a bachelor's degree in childhood education and holds two Master's degrees in both child education and social work. As both a pastor's wife and a minister herself, she has a heart for raising children with a strong Christian foundation and hopes to instil both Christian morals and good manners to her students to prepare them for their next steps in their primary education.
