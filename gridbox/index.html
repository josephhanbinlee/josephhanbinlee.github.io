Apple Music vs. Spotify?

Apple Music and Spotify are both popular music streaming services that many college students often use. In choosing which service to utilize, pricing might be the dealbreaker...until you realize that both services offer students at $4.99/month. Need to decide which service is best for your taste? This infographic is just for you!

What makes Apple Music better?

  • Easier integration with iTunes
  • Exclusive content
  • Bigger catalog of music

What makes Spotify better?

  • Better curated playlists
  • Better integration with other social media platforms
  • Cleaner interface
